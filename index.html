What is Ecological Jurisprudence?

This is an approach to law that recognizes humans are part of Earth's systems and challenges traditional frameworks that treat nature as property. It encompasses Rights of Nature, Indigenous laws, responsibility-based governance, and policies informed by ecological science and Traditional Ecological Knowledge.
