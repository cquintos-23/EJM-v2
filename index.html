Ecological jurisprudence is a global movement that challenges human-centric legal frameworks. It centers the interdependence and intrinsic value of nature and encompasses Rights of Nature, Indigenous Models, and Ecological Governance.

The Eco Jurisprudence Monitor tracks this movement.
